# Public Repository Context

All repositories under this directory are public. When creating issues, PRs, commits, design docs, or other documentation:

## Guidelines

- Focus on user value and technical merit
- Avoid competitor names, especially in negative contexts
- Omit internal business strategy and rationale
- Write for external contributors who lack organizational context
- Keep language professional and welcoming to open-source contributors
- Only reference issues, PRs, and designs in public repos
- Only link to public resources (no private repo links)
- Never mention internal tooling or workflows (`/tsukumogami:explore`, `/tsukumogami:work-on`, `/tsukumogami:plan`, etc.)

## Tone and Style

- Clear explanations without assuming internal knowledge
- Professional but approachable
- Explain "why" for non-obvious decisions
- Be helpful to first-time contributors
